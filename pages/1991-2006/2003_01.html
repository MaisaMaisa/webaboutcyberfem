**workshops with theoretical approach, discussions, more queer perspective + practice = filming each other, making their own porn

We have a name that’s a joke – girlswholikeporno. Girls who like porno, how amusing. But what’s so amusing about it? What’s so amusing in affirming that there are women who like pornography? True, the porno industry has done a lot of harm – an industry full of stereotypes and sexist cliches that has left its mark on us, an industry whose absolute banality has made us loathe it. The term pornography has been contaminated to the extreme. But pornography can be something else. And it is in our hands that it be something else. In our hands and in our cameras. Because if we don’t like what we see, we can film something different. Do it yourself.

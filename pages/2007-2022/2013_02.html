Hashtag Feminism by Tara L. Conley

https://www.hashtagfeminism.com/

**project of love born out of love for internet culture + passion for social justice

**Digital platform that “collects receipts” on Twitter

**Functions to reflect the culture and politics of the hashtag activism era

**The website was hacked

**Revived by a Columbia professor and his students who hosted a #Feminism hack-a-thon to try and help retrieve some of the content

**But the original was gone
